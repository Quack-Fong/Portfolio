Revenge of Goofy Gumball

This is my Scratch Project my partner and I worked on in our CSE class. We came up with this idea from brainstorming several game ideas and narrowed them down by our skillsets. The objective of our project was to make a fun game. We documented this all in our design notebook. Since David, my partner, had experience with side-scrolling games on Scratch, we decided to do a side-scrolling adventure game. From there, we came up with this gumball thing and created a story where these red squares stole gumball's kids and gumball is trying to get them back. We were graded on this project through a rubric and instructions you can find here. This project really helped me grow as a programmer and introduced me to the world of game development.
